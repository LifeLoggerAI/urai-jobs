# URAI-Jobs: AI Governance Policy

## 1. Preamble

The purpose of this document is to ensure that the artificial intelligence (AI) systems within `urai-jobs` are developed and operated in a manner that is safe, fair, effective, and aligned with our commitment to ethical hiring. Our guiding principle is **"AI-Assisted, Human-Led."** AI is a tool to support and augment human judgment, never to replace it.

## 2. Core Principles

### 2.1. Human-in-the-Loop
All final hiring decisions (e.g., advancing a candidate, rejecting a candidate) are made by a human administrator. The AI system is explicitly prohibited from making autonomous negative determinations about a candidate. Its role is to provide summaries and scores to aid decision-making, not to make the decisions itself.

### 2.2. Fairness & Bias Mitigation
We will be proactively vigilant in identifying and mitigating biases related to gender, ethnicity, age, educational institution, and other protected characteristics. System design will prioritize objective, job-related skills and experience over proxies for pedigree. The AI scoring model will be "blind" to all personally identifiable and demographic information.

### 2.3. Transparency & Explainability
The system's logic for scoring and summarization must be understandable and auditable. We will prioritize deterministic, rule-based scoring models over opaque "black box" approaches. When AI-generated content (such as a summary) is displayed, it will be clearly labeled as such. The basis for any given score will be programmatically available for review.

### 2.4. Accountability
We, the creators and operators of `urai-jobs`, are accountable for the system's impact and outcomes. This includes regular monitoring, auditing, and a commitment to rectify issues that arise. An immutable audit trail will log all significant AI-driven events and human actions.

### 2.5. Data Privacy
Candidate data is handled with the utmost respect and security. The AI system will only have access to the minimum data necessary to perform its function (e.g., the text of a resume for a specific job application). All data is subject to our strict data retention and privacy policies, including mechanisms for consent and user-initiated deletion.

## 3. Oversight & Review

This policy and the performance of the AI system will be reviewed on a quarterly basis. The review will include a statistical analysis of the bias audit logs and system accuracy metrics (e.g., correlation between AI scores and human hiring decisions). All changes to the scoring model's weighting or logic will be documented and version-controlled.
