# URAI-JOBS Vendor Risk Management Policy

## 1. Vendor Inventory
Maintain list of:

- Cloud providers
- Payment processors
- Email providers
- Monitoring services

## 2. Risk Classification
- **High Risk:** Data processors
- **Medium Risk:** Infrastructure
- **Low Risk:** SaaS tools without data access

## 3. Due Diligence
For High Risk vendors:

- SOC 2 report requested
- Security questionnaire
- Data processing agreement

## 4. Annual Review
Re-evaluate vendors annually.
